Rooftop solar is the visible half of a home energy story. The quiet half is **everything that uses less electricity in the first place**: insulation, air sealing, efficient appliances, and shifting hot water or HVAC demand away from peak hours when tariffs bite.

Smaller loads mean a smaller inverter and fewer modules for the same net-zero ambition, which can improve payback. It also leaves more export headroom on a constrained service if your utility caps system size.
